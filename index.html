What is FeWo-direct?

FeWo-direct is your competent contact for vacations in a holiday home or holiday apartment. As part of Vrbo, one of the leading online marketplaces for holiday home rentals, FeWo-direct offers you more than two million unique accommodations, including fincas, bungalows, apartments, cottages and even villas. Secure payments, 24/7 support and the Book-with-Confidence Guarantee make bookings child's play. With the user-friendly website and numerous practical filters, you can find your dream holiday apartment for your budget without much effort. This way, you'll definitely spend an unforgettable vacation that is stress-free from the search through booking to your stay and departure.

Renting a holiday apartment or holiday home: These are the advantages

Families and groups especially appreciate the advantages of holiday accommodations: more space, more privacy, more amenities. You have more flexibility and freedom to plan your vacation - regardless of whether you want to go hiking in the picturesque countryside or take a city trip or relax with your dog in the beautiful nature. Looking for a cozy cottage in the mountains with a washing machine and whirlpool? A beach house by the water or a large finca in the south with pool and fully equipped kitchen? No problem, because holiday accommodations come in all sizes and with any desired amenities that you want for your next family vacation, adventure trip with friends or a romantic weekend.
